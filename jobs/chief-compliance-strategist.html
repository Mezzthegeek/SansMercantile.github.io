About the Role

As Chief Compliance Strategist, you will be instrumental in establishing Sans Mercantile’s AI governance strategy and ensuring global regulatory alignment. You’ll architect compliance frameworks across SEC, FINRA, MiFID II, GDPR, ISO/IEC 27001, and Basel III—helping define institutional standards in AI-fintech transparency, integrity, and trust.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement AI governance and algorithmic compliance frameworks for institutional oversight.
  • Ensure compliance with SEC Rule 613, MiFID II trade surveillance, and FINRA advisory mandates.
  • Oversee GDPR, ISO/IEC 27001, and Basel III alignment for financial data governance and security.
  • Lead quarterly audit preparation, execution risk analysis, and cross-border regulatory documentation.
  • Advise the executive team on compliance risk, liability, and ethical AI deployment standards.
  • Collaborate with product and engineering teams to embed “compliance-by-design” across platform features.

Required Qualifications

  • 8+ years of experience in financial compliance, ideally in AI-fintech, banking, or hedge fund environments.
  • Deep working knowledge of SEC, FINRA, MiFID II, GDPR, ISO/IEC 27001, and Basel III frameworks.
  • Proven ability to build institutional-grade compliance architectures across international jurisdictions.
  • Strong familiarity with AI governance principles and risk mitigation for automated financial systems.
  • Exceptional analytical, audit, and executive communication skills.
