About the Role

As our Growth & Community Lead, you will be on the ground, driving adoption and building strong community engagement within high-growth, underserved markets. This role is crucial in democratizing access to AI-driven finance, directly impacting the lives and economic potential of communities often overlooked by traditional financial systems. You will be a key advocate for Sans Mercantile's mission, fostering trust and enabling equitable financial access through our tokenized, mobile-first infrastructure.

Key Responsibilities

  • Identify and onboard early users and community partners in target township regions.
  • Develop and execute grassroots strategies to drive platform adoption and user engagement.
  • Build strong relationships with local community leaders, organizations, and financial collectives.
  • Gather feedback from the community to inform product development and localization efforts.
  • Organize and lead educational workshops and events on AI-driven finance and digital literacy.
  • Act as a brand ambassador, representing Sans Mercantile's mission and values within the community.

Required Qualifications

  • Proven experience in community development, grassroots marketing, or social impact initiatives, ideally in emerging markets.
  • Strong understanding of the unique financial needs and challenges of underserved communities.
  • Excellent interpersonal, communication, and storytelling skills.
  • Ability to build trust and rapport quickly with diverse groups.
  • Self-motivated, adaptable, and passionate about financial inclusion and technology for good.
  • Fluency in local languages relevant to target regions (e.g., isiZulu, Xhosa, etc. for SA).
