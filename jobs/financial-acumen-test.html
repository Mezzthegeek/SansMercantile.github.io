Financial Acumen Test

Focus: Capital structuring, pricing logic, investor alignment.

Duration: 60 minutes | 15 Questions

Time Left: 60:00

Questions

A startup requires $10 million in funding. They are considering two options: 1) 70% equity, 30% debt with an 8% interest rate, or 2) 50% equity, 50% debt with a 10% interest rate. Assuming both have the same valuation considerations for equity, which option likely has a lower Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC) if the cost of equity is 15% and the tax rate is 25%?

Your new AI-trading platform service costs $50,000 per year to develop and maintain for each client. You want to achieve a 20% profit margin. What should be the minimum annual subscription fee per client?
