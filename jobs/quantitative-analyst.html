Quantitative Analyst

Global Remote | Full-time

About the Role

Sans Mercantile is seeking a brilliant and highly motivated Quantitative Analyst to join our pioneering team. In this role, you will be at the heart of our AI-driven trading strategy, developing and implementing sophisticated mathematical models that power our platform. You will work with vast datasets, cutting-edge machine learning techniques, and a world-class team of engineers and data scientists to solve some of the most challenging problems in finance.

Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and test quantitative models for alpha generation, risk management, and portfolio optimization.
  • Analyze large, complex financial datasets to identify statistical patterns and market opportunities.
  • Collaborate with ML engineers to deploy models into our live trading environment.
  • Conduct rigorous backtesting and performance analysis of trading strategies.
  • Stay at the forefront of academic and industry research in quantitative finance and machine learning.
  • Contribute to the continuous improvement of our research and trading infrastructure.

Qualifications

  • PhD or Master's degree in a quantitative field (e.g., Mathematics, Statistics, Physics, Computer Science, or Engineering).
  • Proven experience in developing quantitative models for financial markets.
  • Strong programming skills in Python and proficiency with data science libraries (e.g., NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn).
  • Deep understanding of statistical modeling, time-series analysis, and machine learning techniques.
  • Experience with large-scale data analysis and high-performance computing is a plus.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and a passion for empirical research.
