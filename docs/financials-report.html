Financial Projection & Report

This document presents a comprehensive financial analysis and five-year projection for Sans Mercantile, outlining our revenue models, growth strategies, and risk mitigation.

Our revenue model combines subscription-based advisory services, algorithmic trading profits, and enterprise partnerships, ensuring scalability and sustainable financial growth.

2. Revenue Streams and Projections

Phase 1: Foundation (Year 1)

  • Revenue primarily from consulting, beta subscriptions, and early fintech partnerships.
  • AI model refinement and risk analysis validation continue.
  • Projected revenue: $500K - $1M.

Phase 2: AI Deployment and Market Positioning (Year 2-3)

  • Growth in subscription-based expert advisory.
  • Institutional investors onboarding automated AI-driven trading models.
  • Projected revenue: $3M - $7M, driven by AI trading revenues and fintech integrations.

Phase 3: Market Expansion and Scale (Year 4-5)

  • Large-scale AI-driven trading and global institutional partnerships.
  • Expansion into hedge funds, brokerage firms, and high-net-worth investor portfolios.
  • Projected revenue: $12M - $25M, with strong net profitability.
