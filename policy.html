Privacy Policy & User Guidelines

This document outlines Sans Mercantile’s User Policies, ensuring clear expectations, ethical usage, and compliance for users accessing the AI-driven fintech trading platform. By using the Sans Mercantile platform, users acknowledge and agree to the policies defined herein.

1. Acceptable Use Policy

1.1 Authorized Activities

  • AI-powered market analysis and financial forecasting
  • Institutional trading execution aligned with regulatory compliance
  • Strategic portfolio risk management using AI-driven liquidity tracking models
  • Integration of hedge fund capital deployment models within structured AI-fintech environments

1.2 Prohibited Activities

  • Market manipulation, unauthorized algorithmic trading, or high-frequency execution fraud
  • Reverse engineering, extracting, or replicating AI-trading models for commercial resale
  • Using AI-driven insights for illicit or non-compliant financial activities
  • Circumventing security protocols or attempting unauthorized access to hedge fund trading data

1.3 Institutional Account Compliance

Financial institutions and hedge funds utilizing Sans Mercantile’s AI-trading execution models must:

  • Obtain enterprise-level licensing for AI-fintech platform integration
  • Ensure compliance with SEC, FINRA, and MiFID II regulatory trade execution standards
  • Adhere to liquidity tracking protections and institutional financial governance frameworks

2. Data Privacy & Security Measures

2.1 Institutional Data Protection Policies

Sans Mercantile ensures:

  • User data encryption aligned with global financial cybersecurity mandates
  • AI-powered trade tracking security compliance using blockchain-integrated audit models
  • Strict adherence to ISO/IEC 27001 cybersecurity protocols for hedge fund execution data

2.2 AI-Trading Platform Security Safeguards

Users are prohibited from:

  • Bypassing authentication mechanisms or altering data processing algorithms
  • Utilizing AI-fintech execution models for unauthorized financial transactions
  • Extracting AI-generated financial analytics for redistribution without institutional licensing approvals

3. User Responsibilities & Legal Accountability

3.1 Individual and Institutional Compliance

Users are responsible for:

  • Maintaining ethical trading practices and financial governance standards within AI-advisory frameworks

  • Preventing regulatory violations and maintaining compliance records within institutional portfolios

3.2 Expanded Liability Clauses

Sans Mercantile assumes no responsibility for:

Users acknowledge that institutional oversight mechanisms:

  • Do not guarantee hedge fund profitability or capital growth returns
  • Remain subject to algorithmic volatility and AI-driven liquidity tracking adjustments
  • Require compliance with multi-market financial governance standards to ensure transparency

3.3 Institutional AI-Trading Compliance Governance Measures

Financial institutions utilizing AI-powered hedge fund execution models must:

  • Submit structured compliance verification reports demonstrating algorithmic trade integrity
  • Maintain hedge fund risk governance tracking across automated liquidity adaptation frameworks
  • Ensure AI-trading execution models align with SEC, FINRA, and MiFID II regulatory mandates

Financial institutions must:
